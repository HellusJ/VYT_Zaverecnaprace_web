Last life (Out soon)

Check out trailer now

The recently released game "Last life" is a 2D platformer available for free on Steam. Players find themselves in the midst of a zombie apocalypse, taking on the role of a brave scientist tasked with protecting the last flower on Earth. This rare plant represents hope for the restoration of life, and the scientist must guard it against hordes of undead determined to destroy it. The game offers thrilling levels filled with obstacles, traps, and enemies that players must overcome using their skills and quick reflexes. The graphics are stylized and retro, giving the game a nostalgic feel reminiscent of old arcade games. "Last life" captivates with its atmosphere, engaging gameplay, and touching story of hope and survival.The Steam community has already embraced the game with enthusiasm, praising its innovative approach to the genre. Join the scientist on his epic mission and see if you can save the last piece of life on a devastated planet.
